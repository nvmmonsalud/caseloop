## Title

CaseFlow — Think Before the Room Speaks | OpenAI Build Week

## Description

CaseFlow is an AI-native case-method learning workspace that helps students form an independent, evidence-backed judgment before class and helps faculty turn anonymized cohort reasoning into a better discussion plan.

Built for OpenAI Build Week in the Education category. GPT-5.6 powers five bounded, structured learning workflows: Socratic coaching, preparation-brief generation, cohort analysis, discussion planning, and reflection comparison. Codex served as the engineering partner across product shaping, implementation, synthetic data, persistence and privacy controls, testing, deployment verification, and submission preparation.

Try the credential-free demo: https://caseloop-zeta.vercel.app/demo

Source and setup: https://github.com/nvmmonsalud/caseloop

All demo learners and records are fictional and synthetic. CaseFlow does not perform automated high-stakes grading.
